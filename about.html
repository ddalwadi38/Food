Meet The Capricious Cook

Shreya Patel is a self taught baker who just realised that cooking is her passion and that it is what she wants to do all her life. An ardent learner, she keeps trying new recipes and techniques while learning and gaining experience all the time. She believes that cooking is no less than meditation and has always been her go to whenever she finds herself clueless.

A complete drama queen and while not cooking, you can always find her stuck to her cell phone watching thrillers and drama. An animal lover, she finds great happiness in cooking for people and being the reason behind the smiles on their faces.


Home Baked Goodness!

Freshly baked, straight out of the oven. Only for you!

Oats & Jaggery Cookies

Healthy cookies... Seems like something is wrong here isn't it? But it is not! These are absolutely perfect for when you are on a diet and have cravings for cookies. Made with oatmeal, whole wheat flour, jaggery, almonds and a dash of nutmeg, these are going to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting for more.

Chocolate Cookies With Gooey Caramel Centres

Oooohh chocolate!! Smooth, luscious dark chocolate ganache oozing out beautifully from these rich, soft Chocolate cookies.... Chocolate is the ultimate love!!

Gluten Free Nan-e-Berenji

These traditional Persian cookies are made entirely from Rice flour and are gluten free!! These are light and very different from the traditional cookies with strong flavours. These have a mild rose flavour and are perfect for when you want a change and are looking for something new.
